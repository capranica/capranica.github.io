Music doesn't have any limit for Francine
and this shows in her career

 

    

 

Born in France, Francine started her career as a singer songwriter in Martinique where she is originally from. She has been on the Music International Scene since 1980. Francine is a gifted and talented singer. Her unique vocal style brought her into contact with many of the world finest musicians, bandleaders and artists such as:

   
 
 

THE FRANCINE LUCE’S PROJECTS ~ LOUIS MOHOLO ~ LOUIS MOHOLO’S Septet, Viva La Black and 26 pieces band Dedication Orchestra ~ LESTER BOWIE ~ PULE PHETO ~ JASON YARD ~ LOZ SPEYER’S Rare Mix ~ EVAN PARKER ~ ALEX KOKOSKY ~ STEVE BERESFORD and the Steve Beresford Ensemble ~ PAT THOMAS ~ ALEX MAGUIRE ~ THËBE LIPERE ~ JOËLLE LEANDRE ~ MARK BOUKOUYA ~ MAGGIE NICOLS ~ LUTHER FRANCOIS ~ JAKY and ALEX BERNARD and many others.

The Dance and Theatre Companies: FIN WALKER DANCE ~ PAN PROJECT ~ PUBLIC DREAMERS ~ composer BEN PARK ~ poet JOBY BERNABE.

    
       
 

Always inspired and inspiring others with her spiritual belief, Francine currently sings for the OKI DO YOGA, based in Italy and the WOMEN'S FEDERATION FOR WORLD PEACE, based in London, helping to support love, peace and harmony throughout the world.

    
 

Francine is well known for her skill at redefining the voice. For her, the voice is a versatile instrument which can be reshaped according its purpose: for example, she can sing “straight” with the warmth, range and vibrancy of a great jazz or blues vocalist and, in a moment, alter her voice to create pure sounds drawn from nature and life’s experience. It is this special blend of song and sound which makes Francine's treatment of the voice so unique.

As a songwriter Francine creates innovative compositions which are highly colourful, rhythmic pieces linking groove, jazz with free improvisation, using melodies on different languages French Creole and English.

In her performances Francine finds expression not only through her voice but also through her body – every note, every sound has a physical consequence turning each performance into an aural-visual event.

Nothing is static with Francine. As life and Nature, each day has a new dawn, and so it is that each performance with Francine is a new experience with a life of its own.
