Francine Luce to appear in Jarvis Cocker's Meltdown at the South Bank on June 20 th

Francine Luce will appear at the South Bank contributing to Jerry Dammers' tribute to Sun Ra as part of the Meltdown Festival. She will perform a couple of Sun Ra's compositions arranged by J. Dammers with his SPATIAL A.K.A ORCHESTRA.

The concert will take place at the Queen Elizabeth Hall on Wednesday 20 th June 2007 at 7.45pm. Don't miss it!

Booking information can be found at www.southbankcentre.co.uk
