Look in ~ Look out
 

I offer a variety of workshops focusing on specific aspects of singing. Each workshop welcomes 8 - 12 people and will focus on individual and collective work. Singers with little or no experience through to professionals will be supported in a safe and supportive environment to develop their own full potential. The dynamics and combination of levels of experience are well suited for voice work. All singers and voices are welcome.

Elements covered include:
- Body work
- Grounding and focus
- Relaxation
- Breathing practise
- Movement and voice
- Group singing
- Solo singing
- Performance skills
- Free improvisation
- Lyrics study
- The use of inner feelings in song interpretation
- Openness of the heart
- The joy and love of singing
- Developing creativity
- Developing spontaneity
- Self-expression
- Self-confidence
- Open expression of emotion in front of others
- Connect with your heart
- Guided meditation
- Healing
- Giving and receiving feedback
