What is spiritual healing?
At certain times in our lives we need guidance to fulfil our potential. Healing is the gift of well being. A point of balance in our mind, body and spirit. Healing takes place on a physical, mental, emotional and spiritual level. The healer channels Universal energy thought the hands. He /she can be in contact with or off the body. The healer can’t guarantee healing. Healing is a process and the client has an active part in the healing. The effect of the healing depends on the client’s belief and willingness to heal. Healing requires no race, class, sex, or religious or spiritual belief.

What can you expect from the healing session?
The first session will last for one hour and 15 minutes which will include time to fill in a confidential registration, giving a brief personal and medical history and reasons for seeking healing. The session starts with a discussion to check how the client feels and again at the end to discuss the healing experience and to make an agreement for further healing sessions if appropriate. There are no set rules to how healing should happen. Everyone’s life is unique so the way people experience healing is unique to the individual and can vary from one session to another. The client remains fully clothed at all times and will receiving a chair healing (sitting down position) or a couch healing (lying down position). During healing the client might be aware of the energies, feel a tingling, some warmth or cold, have an emotional release, a physical ease, feel energised or they might feel nothing at all. Feeling nothing does not reveal if healing took place, or whether the healing energy was effective. The healer will keep a record of the healing and will make confidential notes after each session. These notes will be available for the client to look at. Follow up sessions will last 1 hour.

About me
I started helping others to heal during their voice training by helping them to their build self-confidence and empower themselves. I put my hands on my students while teaching them to sing, to help them to relax or release tension, with amazing results. I went on to train in Shiatsu from 1990 - 92 and later trained in healing. I became a fully accredited healer in 2004 with The College of Psychic Studies. I am fully insured and qualified to practice healing and I follow the code of conduct from the Confederation of Healing Organisations based on the accordance with the Code of Conduct and guidance to practitioners issued by the British Complementary Medicine Association.
