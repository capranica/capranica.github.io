Sing and Reach Your Heart

4 week course
2 hours class per week
From level 1, progressing onto levels 2 and 3

    

COURSE OUTLINE

Listen and follow the voice of your heart

    


What is the course about?
Singing starts from within. The aim of the course is to help you to discover your emotions and connect with your heart while singing. Learn to judge your own singing positively and give yourself permission to enjoy the sound of your own voice. Set your voice free as a result. Learn to trust yourself and find the courage to reach a deeper place inside. Fear and worry often get in the way. This course will enable you to ground yourself, relax and breathe effectively. You will find a safe place inside. You will learn to listen, feel and then connect to your feelings and sing your favourite song with true meaning. You will gain the confidence to express your emotions freely through your voice in front of others and learn to trust and be spontaneous. The basis of this workshop is developing the spirit and gift of singing. This is not a technical course, but I will refer to vocal technique as is necessary.

Who is this course for?
This course is for singers who are ready to connect with their emotions through singing, who have a desire to sing from the heart and who are ready to take some risks. The course is suitable for people who have had previous singing experience, semi-professional, and professional singers are welcome. An interest in personal development work is necessary.

What do you need to bring?
In the first week you will need to bring a note book and a pen. For the second week, you will be asked to prepare a song to sing unaccompanied. Throughout the following weeks you will be asked to prepare exercises and songs.

Wear loose and comfortable clothing.
