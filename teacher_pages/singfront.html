One day workshop

    

The opportunity to take the step - To achieve your goals

    

Do you feel apprehensive or scared of singing in front of others? Do you feel that you are holding back when you sing in front of others? Does your heart race? Do your nerves have control of you? Would you like a supportive environment to learn to feel more at ease when you sing in front of others?

What is the workshop about?
The aim of this workshop is to help build self-confidence and self-belief to overcome the barriers that prevent you from singing freely in front of others. Transform your fear or nerves into passion and openness in front of others. Learn to love the sound you make when you sing. Enjoy expressing yourself. Learn to reveal more of your true self while being watched and listened to. You will find out that many others share similar feelings about using their voice. Overcoming your fear teaches you to value yourself and recognise the value that singing can bring to you. Come along and find the support you need. Make your voice heard!

Who is the workshop for?
Singing experience is not necessary. This workshop is suitable or anyone with little or no experience right through to semi professional and professional singers.

What do you need to bring?
Bring a song you want to sing front of others. Learn the words by heart and the tune as best you can. Sheet music is not needed as singers will sing unaccompanied. You are welcome to bring your own backing track or your own instrument if you wish to accompany yourself.

Wear loose and comfortable clothing.
