One day workshop

    

Free Yourself - Free Your Voice

    


What is the workshop about?
This workshop will give you the opportunity to develop personally and creatively. It will encourage you to sing freely in a group and individual situation. You will learn to open up your voice by exploring and experimenting with melodic and free vocal sounds. You will have the chance to expand your vocal possibilities and use your potential, strengthen your voice and project your sound better. You will develop your creativity and spontaneity, discover your hidden sounds and build up self-confidence. Vocal exercises will be accompanied by simple movements - the voice flows and frees up when the whole body is involved. You will find out how singing can be of personal value and nourishment for yourself, as you free your voice and personal expression.

Who is this workshop for?
This workshop is suitable for all levels of experience. No experience is necessary, beginners and those lacking in confidence are welcome.

What do you need to bring?
Just bring yourself! I will be teaching a song during the day for everyone to sing together. The focus is on group work in this workshop.

Wear loose and comfortable clothing.
