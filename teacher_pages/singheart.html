What is the workshop about?
Having a good voice is not enough… Mean what you sing!
In this workshop you will learn to use your inner feelings creatively and connect to your joy of singing. Learning how to ground yourself and finding a safe place inside will enable you to sing your favourite song to others, express your voice from your heart, and release the emotions pictured by the lyric. Singing a song can be compared to telling a story, you will learn to make the song your own by expressing the lyric with conviction, belief and confidence.

Who is it for?
This workshop is for beginners to professional singers who want to discover the art of putting their emotion into their sound and overcome their fear of connecting to and using emotion. Anyone who wants to learn to be creative and discover their vocal freedom and joy of singing.

What do you need to bring?
Choose a song and learn it by heart. Bring a note book, a pen and 2 copies of the lyric. Sheet music is not necessary, songs will be sung unaccompanied.

Wear loose and comfortable clothing.
